Apple Crumble

Home

Description

The apple crumble recipe is easy to make with Granny Smith apples and a buttery crumb topping with cinnamon and nutmeg for a cozy fall dessert.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Spices
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place the apple pieces in a baking dish, then sprinkle with the lemon-water mixture.
  2. Combine the sugar, flour, spices, and salt in another bowl. Add the butter.
  3. Pour the flour mixture over the apples and bake until bubbly.
