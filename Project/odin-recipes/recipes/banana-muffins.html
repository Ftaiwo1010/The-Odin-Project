Banana Muffins

Home

Description

Banana muffins are moist and delicious. This is an easy recipe for kids to help make and perfect for using up ripe bananas. They're wonderful as-is or with walnuts or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Banana
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together in one bowl. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  2. Fold the dry mixture into the wet mixture.
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin tins. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven.
